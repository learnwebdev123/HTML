My Favorite Hobby

Why I Love It

I enjoy playing Badminton because it allows me to relax. There's something incredibly satisfying about transforming a fat body into a lean one. Badminton helps me to unwind and de-stress, and I love exploring different styles and techniques.

How to Get Started

  1. Gather your supplies (Bat, NonMarking Shoes, Feather Cock).
  2. Find a comfortable Badminton Court with good lighting.
  3. Start with simple techniques or play with professionals.
  4. Practice regularly and don't be afraid to experiment new techniques.
